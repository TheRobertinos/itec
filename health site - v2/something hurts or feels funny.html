HEADACHE

Headaches are a very common condition that most people will experience many times during their lives. Some causes are as follows:

- Eating certain foods or ingredients, like caffeine, alcohol, fermented foods, chocolate and cheese

- Exposure to allergens

- Secondhand smoke

- Strong odors from household chemicals or perfumes

CHEST PAIN

Chest pain is not something to ignore. But you should know that it has many possible causes. Here are some of the more common causes of chest pain.

- Heart Problems

- Lung Problems

- Gastrointestinal Problems

- Bone, Muscle, or Nerve Problems

- Another potential cause of chest pain is anxiety and panic attacks

BACK PAIN

Back pain is one of the most common reasons people go to the doctor or miss work, and it is a leading cause of disability worldwide. Here are some causes of back pain:

- Muscle or ligament strain

- Bulging or ruptured disks

- Arthritis

- Osteoporosis

KNEE PAIN

Knee pain is a common complaint among adults and most often associated with general wear and tear from daily activities like walking, bending, standing and lifting. Here are some causes of back pain:

- Sprained or strained knee ligaments and/or muscles

- Torn cartilage

- Tendonitis

- Arthritis
